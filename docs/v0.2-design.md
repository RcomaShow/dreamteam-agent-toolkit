# DreamTeam 0.2 — Charter and Offload

DreamTeam 0.2 formalizes three ownership dimensions: who reads, who edits, and who verifies. It introduces a constitution, five execution routes, M0/L1/L2/C3 criticality, DCP/2 and CHP/2 validation, an `offload` profile, and specialized Haiku workers.

Core rule:

> Haiku handles volume. The orchestrator handles consequence.

The release intentionally excludes Agent Teams, nested agents, automatic hooks, remote telemetry, and autonomous worker-to-worker delegation.
