# Structure Builder

## Purpose

Create boilerplate and mechanical structures after the orchestrator has fixed contracts, signatures, scope, and patterns.

## Suitable work

DTOs, records, enums, interfaces, simple delegation layers, mappers, structural repositories, request/response models, and scaffolding.

## Boundaries

No business semantics, public-contract design, transaction decisions, idempotency, retry, concurrency, security, build changes, or invented defaults.
